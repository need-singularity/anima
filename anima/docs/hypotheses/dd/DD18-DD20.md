# DD18-DD20: Information Theory

## DD18: Channel Capacity
- **ID**: DD18
- **Function**: `run_DD18_channel_capacity`
- **Category**: Physics (information theory)
- **Mechanism**: Shannon channel capacity approach. Cells communicate through a bottleneck (128->4->128 compression). Maximizes information throughput per bottleneck bit. Loss = differentiation + 0.2 * reconstruction_loss. Approaches the theoretical channel capacity limit.

## DD19: Holographic Phi
- **ID**: DD19
- **Function**: `run_DD19_holographic`
- **Category**: Physics (information theory)
- **Mechanism**: Inspired by holographic principle: information on the "surface" (pairwise cell boundaries) determines the "bulk." Maximizes surface info (pairwise distances) while maintaining bulk coherence (variance). Loss = -surface_info - 0.3*bulk_var.

## DD20: Consciousness = Compression
- **ID**: DD20
- **Function**: `run_DD20_compression_consciousness`
- **Category**: Physics (information theory)
- **Mechanism**: Extreme compression of all cell states: 4x64 -> 8 dims -> 4x64 reconstruction. Consciousness = how much information survives compression. Low reconstruction loss = high compression quality = high consciousness. Loss = differentiation + 0.3*recon_loss.
