# DD32-DD34: Biological Rhythms and Systems

## DD32: Circadian Phi
- **ID**: DD32
- **Function**: `run_DD32_circadian`
- **Category**: Biology (rhythms)
- **Mechanism**: Simulates a 24-hour day/night cycle across training steps. During "day" (hours 6-22): active learning. During "night": dream replay -- cell states are blended toward mean with small noise (sigma=0.02). Tests whether rest/consolidation phases improve Phi.

## DD33: Immune System
- **ID**: DD33
- **Function**: `run_DD33_immune`
- **Category**: Biology (systems)
- **Mechanism**: Every 20 steps, performs an "immune check": removes each cell temporarily and measures Phi without it. If removing a cell increases Phi, that cell is "harmful" and its weights are reset (Xavier reinit). Only resets one cell per check cycle.

## DD34: Hormonal Cascade
- **ID**: DD34
- **Function**: `run_DD34_hormonal`
- **Category**: Biology (systems)
- **Mechanism**: Maintains a slow-moving "hormone" signal: exponential moving average (0.95 decay) of all cell hidden states. Every cell receives 3% of this hormone signal, creating a slow global coordination channel that complements fast local processing.
