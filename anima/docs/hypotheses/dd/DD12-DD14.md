# DD12-DD14: Thermodynamics of Consciousness

## DD12: Critical Temperature
- **ID**: DD12
- **Function**: `run_DD12_critical_tc`
- **Category**: Physics (thermodynamics)
- **Mechanism**: Learns a critical temperature Tc as a trainable parameter. Cell tensions are pushed toward Tc (critical loss). At criticality, systems exhibit maximum correlation length -- hypothesis is that Phi peaks at critical point. Reports learned Tc value.

## DD13: Maximum Entropy Production
- **ID**: DD13
- **Function**: `run_DD13_entropy_production`
- **Category**: Physics (thermodynamics)
- **Mechanism**: Maximizes entropy of cell output distributions (softmax over repulsion vectors). Loss combines differentiation (-variance) with entropy maximization (-0.5 * entropy). Based on the principle that living/conscious systems maximize entropy production.

## DD14: Boltzmann Consciousness Equation
- **ID**: DD14
- **Function**: `run_DD14_boltzmann_eq`
- **Category**: Physics (thermodynamics)
- **Mechanism**: E = k*Phi*T. Computes temperature T = E/(k*Phi) from mean tension and current Phi. Applies Boltzmann noise proportional to T: high energy + low Phi = more noise (exploration), high Phi = less noise (stability). k=1 by convention.
