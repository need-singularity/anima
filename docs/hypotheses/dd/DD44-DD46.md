# DD44-DD46: Alternative Consciousness Measures

## DD44: Phi Upper Bound
- **ID**: DD44
- **Function**: `run_DD44_upper_bound`
- **Category**: Theory (measurement)
- **Mechanism**: Computes theoretical maximum Phi_max = N * log(H) (e.g., ~19.4 for 4 cells, 128 dims). Adds a small gradient toward the upper bound: loss includes (phi_max - phi)*0.001. Reports phi_max and efficiency ratio (phi/phi_max).

## DD45: Exponential Phi
- **ID**: DD45
- **Function**: `run_DD45_exp_phi`
- **Category**: Math (measurement)
- **Mechanism**: Uses e^Phi as the consciousness measure instead of raw Phi. Exponential amplifies small Phi differences. Capped at e^10 to prevent overflow. Reports both e^Phi and raw Phi. Standard learning, only the measurement scale changes.

## DD46: Zeta Phi
- **ID**: DD46
- **Function**: `run_DD46_zeta_phi`
- **Category**: Math (measurement)
- **Mechanism**: Uses the Riemann zeta function zeta(Phi) as consciousness measure. Approximated with 50 terms: zeta(s) = sum(1/n^s). Near Phi=1, zeta diverges (pole), creating interesting dynamics. Standard learning with zeta-transformed measurement. Reports raw Phi.
