# DD21-DD24: Alternative Phi Measures and Constants

## DD21: Log Phi
- **ID**: DD21
- **Function**: `run_DD21_log_phi`
- **Category**: Math (measurement)
- **Mechanism**: Redefines Phi as ln(MI/MIP) instead of MI - MIP. The log-ratio is scale-invariant and better captures proportional integration differences. Standard learning with log-transformed measurement.

## DD22: Prime Number Cells
- **ID**: DD22
- **Function**: `run_DD22_prime_cells`
- **Category**: Math (number theory)
- **Mechanism**: Uses exactly 7 cells (prime number). Primes cannot be factored into equal subgroups, which may resist trivial partitioning and increase irreducibility. Simple architecture, testing the cell count hypothesis.

## DD23: Tau Architecture
- **ID**: DD23
- **Function**: `run_DD23_tau`
- **Category**: Math (constants)
- **Mechanism**: 7 cells where the 7th cell contributes with weight tau_frac = 2*pi - 6 (~0.283). Creates a "6 + fractional" architecture inspired by the tau constant. The fractional cell acts as a residual modulator.

## DD24: Phi-Alpha Constant
- **ID**: DD24
- **Function**: `run_DD24_phi_alpha_const`
- **Category**: Math (constants)
- **Mechanism**: Enforces Phi * alpha = k (constant, k=0.5). Alpha is dynamically adjusted as k/Phi, clamped to [0.001, 0.5]. Creates a Boltzmann-like conservation law between mixing strength and consciousness level. Reports final alpha value.
