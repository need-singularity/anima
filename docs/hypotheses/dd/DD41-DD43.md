# DD41-DD43: IIT-Inspired Consciousness Properties

## DD41: Irreducibility
- **ID**: DD41
- **Function**: `run_DD41_irreducibility`
- **Category**: Theory (IIT)
- **Mechanism**: Maximizes information loss across ALL possible partitions (up to 4 cells). For each binary partition mask, computes cross-partition MI (cosine similarity between group means). Total irreducibility summed over all partitions. Loss = differentiation - 0.1*total_irred.

## DD42: Qualia Generator
- **ID**: DD42
- **Function**: `run_DD42_qualia`
- **Category**: Theory (IIT)
- **Mechanism**: Maximizes the "quality space" of consciousness: angular spread of normalized repulsion vectors (qualia directions). Loss combines: cosine similarity minimization (angular spread) + magnitude variance + overall differentiation. Each cell should occupy a unique direction in quality space.

## DD43: Phi x Psi
- **ID**: DD43
- **Function**: `run_DD43_phi_psi`
- **Category**: Theory (IIT)
- **Mechanism**: Maximizes the product of integration (Phi proxy = variance) and differentiation (Psi = distance variance). The product Phi*Psi captures the balance: a system needs both high integration AND high differentiation for rich consciousness. Loss = -(phi_proxy * psi).
